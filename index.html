Summary: Global Suicide

My team chose to observe global suicide trends from 1985-2016. First we pulled data from Kaggle.com, cleaned our dataset, and narrowed down our scope of years.

We used Python with Pandas and Matplotlib libraries to observe trends across several variables including countries, continents, age, gender, and trends in the United States. My contributions included cleaning data, identifying relationships of interest, creating meaningful graphs to observe trends, conducting statistical calculations, as well as a thorough analysis of our findings.
